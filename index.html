Black Existencia Films is an

independent Afro-descendant

audiovisual production company

created on May 11, 2017 by Wisny Dorce, an independent filmmaker and producer with experience in the field since 2014

In Black Existencia Films we promote the socioeconomic inclusion and culture of Afro-descendant immigrants in the world, providing job opportunities for actors, technicians and audiovisual personnel of African descent and African immigrants and generating content to reach the community, transmit messages and thus make visible the realities lived.
